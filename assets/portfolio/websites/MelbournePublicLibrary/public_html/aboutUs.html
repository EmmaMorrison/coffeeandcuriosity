About Melbourne Public Library

Established to provide a public library collection of learning and discovery, the Melbourne Public Library has a simple vision of providing a place for all our visitors to create, connect, learn and discover.

The Melbourne Public Library is a destination for all who want to access our cultural heritage as well as the latest acquisitions and additions to our extensive collection. We not only have books to borrow, but e-books, magazines and e-magazines, DVD's, video games and board games, and even a free music service, giving you access to over 9 million songs!

There is always something new to discover at our regular exhibitions and events that are designed to educate and fascinate all ages on a variety of subjects.

We welcome all who enter, so join us at the library and continue your journey to discovery!
