Become a Library member

Membership is free. Being a library member gives you access to our entire online resource collection. You can also borrow and reserve books through the website, and opt to have them mailed to you. If you sign up online, you will automatically be given a membership number, which will grant you immediate access to the library's online resources. Your card will be mailed within 7 days from registration.
