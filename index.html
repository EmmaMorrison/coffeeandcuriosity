About me

Well, mostly about my journey into web design! I have been a graphic designer for over 15 years, primarily designing and creating for print. Wanting to branch out, and extend my skillset, I started doing online courses in html and css...and became hooked. I enjoy the challenge of coding and solving the problems that crop up when dealing with websites. Being still (relatively) new to this, I'm always learning new things, and having a go at different languages. Some of the ideas I have tried to incorporate into the creation of my website have been, exploring and ensuring some measure of accessibility, and creating a responsive website that scales well to any device screen size.
